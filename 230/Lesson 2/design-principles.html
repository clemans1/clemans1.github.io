Principles of Design

Visual Hierarchy

Walmart

On Walmarts homepage navigation buttons are placed accross the top of the page where the eyes scan first. White space draws more attention to an area and we see that at the bottom of this page "Free Grocery Pickup" is surrounded by white space drawing greater attention to that area. Also, moving objects draw great attention to an area we see that Walmart uses moving images to draw attention to certain offerings avaliable.

Rule of Thirds

Bing

The "Rule of Thirds" states that an image should be split into a grid of 3x3. On the grid line is where the certer of attention should be focused. The photos that Bing uses follows this rule making it more visually appealing to the viewer.

Hick's Law

Ebay

Hicks law states that with every additional choice the time it takes to make a decision increases. Thus, we have a greater need for filters. Here Ebay filters your possible choices by allowing you to chose a category to browse.
